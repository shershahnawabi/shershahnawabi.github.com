Who is Shershah Nawabi:

Shershah Nawabi is an Afghan Journalist. He is working in Afghan mass media for almost 12 years. From print to TV and online media are the paths that Mr. Nawabi has to sacrifice his life. He is also a Journalism lecturer, according to Mr. Nawabi: "Mass Media and journalism can create critics and form a new generation of critical thinkers, in a country." Mr. Nawabi is an award-winning Journalist who was honored by the presidential second-grade certificate in 2015. he also covered Kunduz fall down one and two; Mr. Nawabi worked as a combat journalist and has served the Afghan land by having trips to at least 24 provinces. Now he is owning his own media group under name of "Pasbanan Media Group" or "PMG". he also works as a freelancer, and he has expertise in live shows, live interviews, writing, and many more. Working in battlegrounds, covering investigative stories, and supporting all Journalists globally by covering Afghanistan stories is the main objective of Mr. Nawabi.
